DDE Store

An app store for DDE built with DTK

Originally built for UbuntuDDE, this app store is designed to replace the official Deepin store on other distros, which the official Deepin store does not support. Built 100% in DTK rather (than a webview like the official store) and getting app data from PackageKit and AppStream means full compatibility on any distro supporting those technologies.
